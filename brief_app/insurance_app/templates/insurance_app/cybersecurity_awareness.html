Stay Safe Online

Protect yourself from cyber threats by staying informed. Learn the best practices to ensure your personal and professional information remains secure.

Secure Your Accounts

Strong passwords are your first line of defense. Use unique, complex passwords for each account and consider using a password manager for added security.

  • Use a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Change passwords regularly.

Avoid Phishing Scams

Phishing emails and messages trick you into revealing sensitive information. Stay cautious and verify the source before clicking on links or downloading attachments.

  • Check email sender addresses carefully.
  • Beware of offers that sound too good to be true.
  • Never share personal information via email.

Keep Your Software Updated

Regular updates fix vulnerabilities that hackers could exploit. Ensure your operating system, browser, and antivirus software are always up to date.
